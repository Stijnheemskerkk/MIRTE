IAm Mirte

The affordable and modular educational robot. Mirte is a robot developed for education. Robotics, due to its multidisciplinary nature, is a good entry point for teaching STEM education. That's why many robotics kits have already been developed. So why another one? We want to make robotics accessible to everyone, with affordability being one of the key principles. It also had to be a robot that you can continue to benefit from. That's why Mirte is designed to remain challenging and expandable from elementary school to university. Step by step to the next level in each discipline: hardware, electrical engineering, and software. Everything is in layers and the difficulty level is easy to build up.

Read more about it in the downloadable brochure.

IamMirte makes Mirte even more inclusive. We do this by covering the entire chain with social enterprises. We're not there yet, but we've started with the social enterprises Printplezier, iamIT, and Fier fietskoeriers. We do this in close collaboration with TU Delft, which developed Mirte and supports us in this process.

For questions and/or comments, you can always contact us at info@iammirte.org.
