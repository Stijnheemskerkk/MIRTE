About Us

I Am Mirte (IamMirte) has officially been a foundation since July 14, 2023 (Chamber of Commerce number 90828658).

The current foundation board has been working on it behind the scenes for much longer.

The board consists of:

Treasurer: Ellen Pennings
Secretary: Martin Klomp
Chairman: Michel van Leeuwen

Our goal in short:

"Promoting technical education, including robotics and computer science, with the aim of making the last two techniques more accessible in a sustainable way"
